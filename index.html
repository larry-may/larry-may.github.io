I am a game studies, digital cultures and digital learning researcher and teacher currently employed at the University of Auckland, New Zealand. My research typically seeks to understand how people come to generate and tell different kinds of stories while playing videogames or engaging with new media, and my teaching expertise encompasses digital games, media and communications theory and practice, and learning design.

I also provide curriculum development and digital learning leadership in my current role, and I’m driven by the great things that happen when you connect the right people and collaborate on interesting answers to difficult problems.

I am always interested and eager to collaborate with other passionate people – so please feel free to contact me – whether by email, through the University,  on Twitter, or on LinkedIn – if that sounds like you.
