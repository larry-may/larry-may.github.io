Educator

I am a lecturer in the University of Auckland’s Faculty of Education and Social Work, with expertise in digital pedagogies, videogames, technology-enhanced learning, twenty-first century learning spaces and learning design. I also provide strategic advice and decision-making in relation to digital learning, with a leadership role as Curriculum Development Manager.

I’ve designed and lectured curricula related to video games, interactive narrative, critical media theory and creative software development. I’ve also designed and managed the delivery of a diploma-to-degree bridging programme, and worked as part of a small development team to take a Bachelor of Creative Software degree to approval.

Previously I led a team of creative, innovative and driven learning designers building digital learning and teaching capacity within the Faculty of Education and Social Work. Before that, as a learning designer, I spent several years immersed in the possibilities opened up by mobile learning and next-generation learning environments, and my expertise also extends to playful learning, online teaching, and innovative approaches to learning design itself.

You can learn a lot more about my professional experience by visiting my LinkedIn profile.
